# ranch dressing

## Ingredients

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1/2 teaspoon dried chives
- 1/2 tespoon dried parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon dired dill weed
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper


## Instructions

- mix
- cool
- serve
